# Kantaka Sodhana LMS — Now Live!

Your all-in-one platform for the Data Science Residency Program is ready at **kantaka-sodhana.app/lms**

---

## What You Can Do

**Dashboard** — Your daily command center with progress, deadlines, and priority tasks at a glance.

**Courses & Curriculum** — Structured modules and lessons across Python, SQL, ML, and more. Track your completion as you go.

**Daily Standups** — Submit what you did, what you're doing, and any blockers. Mentors review these daily.

**Tasks & Assignments** — Manage daily tasks, submit assignments, and receive graded feedback from mentors.

**Quizzes** — Test your knowledge. Scores feed into your learning profile.

**GitHub Integration** — Connect your GitHub and your commits are tracked automatically. Commit regularly!

**Peer Code Reviews** — Review your peers' commits and get feedback on yours. Builds collaboration and better code habits.

**Portfolio & Capstone** — Showcase your projects and track your capstone from proposal to final submission.

**Hackathons** — Participate in batch hackathon events, form teams, and compete.

**Progress Tracking** — See your consolidated performance across tasks, assignments, quizzes, commits, and more.

**Calendar & Notifications** — All deadlines and events in one place. Get notified about reviews, deadlines, and announcements.

**Dark Mode** — Switch to dark theme from Settings > Appearance.

**Mobile Friendly** — Full access from your phone with a slide-out menu.

---

## Quick Tips

- Submit standups before 10 AM daily
- Commit code every day — even small commits count
- Check notifications for peer review requests and deadlines
- Bookmark the dashboard as your daily starting point
- Works on mobile — check tasks or submit standups on the go

Log in at **kantaka-sodhana.app/lms** and explore!
